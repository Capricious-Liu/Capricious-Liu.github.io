About Me

I am currently a Ph.D. candidate in CSE department of Hong Kong University of Science and Technology (HKUST), supervised by Prof. James Kwok. Previously, I was an undergraduate student majoring in Software Engingeering in Tongji University honored as the Outstanding Undergraduate of Shanghai (上海市优秀毕业生).

My current research projects include:

  • (1) Mixture of cluster-conditional experts: MoCLE, MoCE, SDR
  • (2) Model Reasoning & Safety: Atomthink, MoTE, Geom-Erasing, ECSO
  • (3) Omni-modal: EMOVA
